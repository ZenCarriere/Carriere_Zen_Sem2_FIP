AboutSection

Crest Grolsch advanced alcohol free mouth wash is the result of the joint business venture of Crest and Grolsch. Crest's alcohol free mouth wash was in need of a new take as consumers were losing interest. Apparently the issue with the alcohol free mouth wash was the alcohol free part. Consumers clearly want a buzz while they brush. As this would lose most dentists recommendation they partnered with Grolsch to make a beer flavoured alcohol free mouth wash. Now combining the excellent dental benefits of Crest products with the great taste of Grolsch beer.
